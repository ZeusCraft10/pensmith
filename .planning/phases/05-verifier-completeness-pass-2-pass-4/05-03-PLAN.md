<objective>
Implement Pass 4 (per-paragraph orphan-claim audit) as an advisory pass whose CORE (claim extraction + orphan detection) is pure-Node deterministic, modeled on `bin/lib/quote-extractor.ts`. Steps 1 and 2 (sentence-level claim extraction and orphan-vs-cited classification) never call an LLM — they are pure regex/word-count heuristics per PRD §14. Step 3 (advisory edge-case labeling of AMBIGUOUS sentences via the `orphan-label` prompt) is the ONLY LLM seam, gated behind `assertBudget` and the `PENSMITH_NO_LLM` guard. Purpose: VRFY-06. Determinism of claim extraction and orphan detection is the load-bearing correctness property — `tier-contract` parity depends on identical orphan sets on both tiers (and across OSes), which only holds if extraction has zero non-determinism (no NLP library, Pitfall 4).
