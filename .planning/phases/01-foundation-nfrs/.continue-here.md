## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)

- **Lock inside `.paper/`** — OneDrive will eat it on this dev machine. Lock MUST live in `LOCAL_DATA_DIR` (D-08).
- **Skip dir-fsync after rename** — silent-data-loss on power loss / sync pause. The dir-fsync MUST be implemented and covered by a rename-then-crash test (D-04).
- **Direct `fs.writeFile` outside `atomic-write.ts`** — bypasses the chokepoint and the lint rule. All higher-layer state writes go through `writeAtomic()` (D-07).
- **Smuggling later-phase work into Phase 1** — no MCP server, no doctor, no `tier-contract.test.js`, no section state machine, no wave scheduling, no `/pensmith list`. Out-of-scope items are listed in CONTEXT.md `<domain>`.
- **Trusting input DOIs** — normalize on write, not just on read; trailing-punct false-FABRICATED is the #1 source of fake-failures.
